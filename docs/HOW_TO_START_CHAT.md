# How to start chat

## Option 1: From a listing (main way)

1. Open **Home** or **Browse** (listings tab).
2. Tap any **listing card** (e.g. "200 kg matel", "500 matel") to open the **Listing detail** screen.
3. At the **bottom** of the screen you’ll see two buttons:
   - **Chat** (outline, chat bubble icon) – starts a conversation with the **seller** of that listing.
   - **Make Offer** – express interest / make an offer.
4. Tap **Chat** → the chat screen opens with that listing’s seller. Type a message and send.

**Who you’re chatting with:**  
- On **Customer** app: you (customer) chat with the **seller** of the listing (often a dealer).  
- On **Pro** app: you (dealer) chat with the **customer** who created the listing (when they tap Chat on your listing).

---

## Option 2: From Messages (existing conversations)

1. If your app has a **Messages** or **Chat** entry (e.g. in Profile or menu), open it to see **Chat inbox** (list of conversations).
2. Tap a conversation to open that chat and continue messaging.

*(If you don’t see Messages in the app, start chats only from Option 1.)*

---

## Testing Customer ↔ Pro chat

- **AVD (emulator):** Run **Customer** app, log in as customer (e.g. 03001234567).
- **Phone:** Run **Pro** app, log in as dealer (e.g. 03001110004).

**On Customer (AVD):**  
Home or Browse → tap a listing (e.g. one you created) → Listing detail → **Chat**.  
You’re now chatting with the dealer who can see that listing.

**On Pro (phone):**  
Browse listings in your zone → tap a listing (e.g. “200 kg matel”) → Listing detail → **Chat**.  
You’re now chatting with the customer who posted that listing.

Messages go through the backend; both sides must be online and the backend/chat service must be running.
